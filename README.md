This repository contains my project and assignments completed during my CodeAlpha internship.

Objective: Classify iris flowers into three distinct species based on their sepal and petal measurements.
2. Unemployment Analysis
Objective: Analyze and visualize unemployment trends and patterns to identify significant variations in the data.
3. Car Price Prediction
Objective: Build a regression model to accurately estimate the market price of a car based on its technical and physical features.
4. Sales Prediction
Objective: Forecast future sales outcomes by analyzing historical data and trends to support business decision-making.
